# Side Hustle Starter Guide
## 10 Ways to Make Your First $500 Online

Turn this into a PDF in Canva or Google Docs, upload to Gumroad (free, set price to $0), and use that link in your bio page.

---

### 1. Local Business Reel Ads ($150 per client)
Offer 3 short promo reels to a coffee shop, barber, or gym. Film on your phone. Edit in CapCut. One client per week = $600/month.

### 2. Notion / Canva Template ($9 each)
Package something you already use: budget tracker, content calendar, habit tracker. List on Gumroad. Post the problem it solves in Facebook groups.

### 3. Resume + LinkedIn Rewrite ($49)
Help one person per week polish their resume. Use ChatGPT for drafts, you edit and personalize. Post before/after (with permission) on Instagram.

### 4. Subscription Audit Service ($25)
Offer to find and cancel unused subscriptions for busy people. Use a simple checklist. Market to parents and professionals on local Facebook groups.

### 5. Etsy Digital Printables ($5-15)
Quotes, planners, budget sheets. Canva to PDF. Etsy handles payment. One design, unlimited sales.

### 6. User-Generated Content (UGC) for Brands ($50-200/video)
Brands pay for authentic-looking product videos. No followers required. Apply on Billo, JoinBrands, or cold email small Shopify stores.

### 7. Facebook Marketplace Flipping
Buy undervalued furniture or electronics locally. Clean, photograph well, resell at market price. $50-200 profit per flip.

### 8. Task-Based Freelancing
Data entry, transcription, research on Upwork or Fiverr. Start at $15/hr. Complete 5-star reviews, then raise rates.

### 9. Affiliate Content (this Instagram account)
Post daily money tips. Link high-yield savings, budget apps, and tools in bio. Earn commission per signup.

### 10. Weekly "Accountability" Check-In ($20/month)
Offer a 15-minute monthly money check-in via Zoom. 10 clients = $200/month recurring. Post testimonials as you get them.
