Side Hustle Starter Guide

10 ways to make your first $500 online. Save or share this page.

1. Local Business Reel Ads ($150 per client)

Offer 3 short promo reels to a coffee shop, barber, or gym. Film on your phone. Edit in CapCut. One client per week is $600/month.

2. Notion / Canva Template ($9 each)

Package something you already use: budget tracker, content calendar, habit tracker. List on Gumroad. Post the problem it solves in Facebook groups.

3. Resume + LinkedIn Rewrite ($49)

Help one person per week polish their resume. Use AI for drafts, you edit and personalize. Post before/after with permission.

4. Subscription Audit Service ($25)

Find and cancel unused subscriptions for busy people. Market to parents and professionals on local Facebook groups.

5. Etsy Digital Printables ($5-15)

Quotes, planners, budget sheets. Canva to PDF. Etsy handles payment. One design, unlimited sales.

6. UGC for Brands ($50-200/video)

Brands pay for authentic product videos. No followers required. Apply on Billo, JoinBrands, or email small Shopify stores.

7. Facebook Marketplace Flipping

Buy undervalued items locally. Clean, photograph well, resell at market price. $50-200 profit per flip.

8. Task-Based Freelancing

Data entry, transcription, research on Upwork or Fiverr. Start at $15/hr. Get 5-star reviews, then raise rates.

9. Affiliate Content (Instagram)

Post daily money tips. Link savings apps and tools in bio. Earn commission per signup.

10. Monthly Accountability Check-In ($20/month)

15-minute monthly money check-in via Zoom. Ten clients = $200/month recurring.
